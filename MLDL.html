MLDL Lecture series

This learning group is established to organize a weekly lecture by volunteer participants (from, UGPGPhDFaculty) to share and deliver the emerging concepts in the area of Deep learning and machine learning and its applications.
This proposed lecture series is open to everyone who is interested in the area of ML and DL.
Typical agenda of this lecture series is to give a 30 minute talkPresentation on a topic followed by an open discussion and question-answer-suggestion session.

Title: “Hiding Data using deep learning”
Speaker Name: Rohit Gandikota
Affiliation: Alumni IIST & Scientist, NRSC.

Abstract: Explore how one can hide any form of data inside an image in the easiest way possible.
